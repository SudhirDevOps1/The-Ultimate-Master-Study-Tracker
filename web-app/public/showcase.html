Activity Detection & Auto-Pause

FlowTrack monitors your activity and can automatically pause sessions when you're not actively working. This prevents inflated time tracking and ensures accurate study hours.

How It Works

Active Study

You're actively working - typing, clicking, scrolling. Timer runs normally.

Start of Inactivity

No mouse, keyboard, or touch input detected. 10-minute countdown begins.

10 Minutes Elapsed

No activity detected. Session automatically pauses in Strict Focus Mode.

Notification Sent

Desktop notification alerts you that your session was auto-paused.

Resume or Stop

When you return, resume the session or stop it and save your progress.

Accurate Tracking Guaranteed

Enable Strict Focus Mode in Settings to activate 10-minute auto-pause. Only time when you're actively working gets counted toward your study goals.
