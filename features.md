# 🚀 FlowTrack Pro – Future Features Roadmap (Ideas & Proposals)

> **Note:** These are planned future enhancement proposals (Ideas 1, 2, 3, and 5). This file is stored locally for documentation purposes and is not committed/pushed to GitHub.

---

## 🔮 Planned Enhancements

### 1. 🚫 Smart Distraction App Blocker (Hard Lock)
- **Concept:** Automatic detection of distracting apps/games/social media during an active study timer.
- **Behavior:** If a distracting process is focused during study time, FlowTrack Pro will automatically bring itself to front or show an overlay alert to keep you focused.

### 2. 📊 Weekly & Monthly PDF Report Generator
- **Concept:** One-click automated PDF summary generation for long-term progress reviews.
- **Content:** Includes study hours breakdown, productivity score graphs, top used applications, and domain statistics formatted cleanly into a PDF document.

### 3. 🎯 Daily Study Schedule & Time-Table Planner
- **Concept:** Time-block planner for structured daily routines.
- **Functionality:** Set fixed study slots (e.g. *09:00 AM - 11:00 AM: Mathematics*, *02:00 PM - 04:00 PM: Coding*). Automatic system notification triggers when a scheduled slot begins.

### 5. 🏆 Focus Streaks & Weekly Rank Badges
- **Concept:** Enhanced gamification milestones for continuous study habits.
- **Rewards:** Special unlockable badges and XP bonuses for maintaining 7-day, 14-day, and 30-day continuous study streaks.
